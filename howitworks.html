How Snug Pines Orders Work

  1. Choose Your Hoodie – Pick your favourite design and size.
  2. Add to Cart & Order – Click Order and fill in your name and email.
  3. Order Confirmation – After ordering, you’ll see a “Thanks for shopping!” message confirming your order.
  4. In-Person Payment – We’ll reach out to arrange your payment and delivery. All purchases are paid in person.

If you have any questions or special requests, feel free to email us at SnugPines@hotmail.com.
